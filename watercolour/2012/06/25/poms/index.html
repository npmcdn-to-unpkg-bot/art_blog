Steps

source image

This is the source image by another artist.

[full size]

underpainting

A very light, shapeless wash of the primary colours used in the painting:

  • Aureolin
  • Permanent Rose
  • Cobalt Blue

[full size]

negative painting

Using very dark colours, fill in the negative areas of the background to provide shape for the pomegranates and the lighter leaves.

[full size]

final version

I like the effect of negative painting and the shading on the poms to give them quite a lot of dimension.

[full size]
